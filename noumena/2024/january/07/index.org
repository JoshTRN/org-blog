* Good Morning, Noumena!

For the first time in a long time, Noumena serves as my alarm clock. She wakes up at 8:30am and I get out of bed immediately to start preparing her breakfast. She's not whining at all, just talking to herself. I warm her porridge and just as I'm finishing up the talking grows to a slight whine. Once again, Noumena has a messy diaper this morning, so I change her and leave off her bottoms taking her out to enjoy breakfast with me.

[[file:big-bite-at-breakfast.jpg]]

[[file:munching-big-bite-at-breakfase.jpg]]

After breakfast, we read a lot of books. Something I've been noticing lately is Noumena likes to read through a book fully one time but then when she's finished, she likes to go back through and look back at a bunch of the pages. We also get out her flash cards. She's a lot better at putting them in the reader and it seems like she's gotten so good so fast. She has a few obvious favorites. The snake, dinosaur and shark are ones she likes to put in over and over again.
